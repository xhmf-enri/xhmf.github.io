about the book

three learning levels that grow with the child.

The Brainbook is an innovative neuroscience guide for children aged 5–10, featuring interactive augmented reality. Readers can select between three learning levels (ages 5, 7, and 10), offering a unique and adaptable educational experience that grows with the child.

Available in English, Albanian, Italian, and Ukrainian, the book also provides options for background classical music and voice-over narration. The final pages include a puzzle and a coloring section to reinforce learning. With 3D augmented reality bringing illustrations to life, The Brainbook is both an engaging and long-term educational investment.
