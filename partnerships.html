partnerships

stronger together.

We partner with organizations across Kosovo and internationally to expand access to STEM education, research, and healthcare.

since 2025

innovation centre kosovo (ick)

We partnered with the Innovation Centre Kosovo (ICK) to host BrainCamp, our flagship neuroscience education program. ICK played a crucial role by providing state-of-the-art tech infrastructure, a dynamic learning environment, and dedicated marketing support to reach students across Kosovo.

As a hub for entrepreneurship, tech innovation, and digital skills training, ICK empowers youth and startups to drive Kosovo's knowledge economy forward, making them an ideal partner in expanding access to cutting-edge science education.

impact year 2024

kosova's organization of medical students (koms)

KOMS is an organization of students and young professionals in the medical sciences. In 2024, XHMF pledged its support to 14 KOMS medical students, enabling their participation in the internationally recognized Standing Committee on Professional Exchange (SCOPE) program organized by IFMSA.

students sponsored: Edita Saljunovic, Erdi Rexha, Erblia Krasniqi, Qendrim Daci, Ereza Dervishi, Adelisa Dzogovic, Leotrim Berisha, Fatbardh Halitaj, Leonita Berisha, Rrezarta Berbati, Ambla Demi, Loti Shaqiri, Bardh Agaj, and Argjenda Baliqi.

since 2021

atomi institute

The ATOMI Institute for Extraordinary Intelligence aims to identify and support gifted students (IQ above 140) in Kosovo by providing special, tailored opportunities, treatments, and education in accordance with their intellectual potential.

In 2021, XHMF committed to supporting ATOMI on a yearly basis. ATOMI has identified over 286 students, in over 18 provinces, from 3 ethnicities.

impact year 2019

amc health

AMC aims to save lives and improve the health of infants, mothers, and children in the Republic of Kosovo. XHMF supported AMC in developing an educational application to educate physicians and the general public, based on scientific research, about breastfeeding.
