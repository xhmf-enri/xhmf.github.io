events

convening the conversations that move science forward.

From Kosovo's first Neuroscience Next Hub to international conferences, XHMF organizes events that bring world-class science directly to local audiences.

event · 2025

aaic — neuroscience next hub kosovo 2025

feb 25, 2025 9:00 am cet tech park prishtina 60 attendees

In partnership with Dr. Bardhë Agaj, former president of KOMS, we organized Kosovo's first Neuroscience Next Hub, sponsored by the AAIC. We had several invited speakers: Prof. Visar Berisha, Prof. Venet Osmani, Prof. Fekrije Selimi and Dr. Taulant Muka. Additionally, we had an invited plenary speaker, Dr. Jonathan Rosand who also gave a workshop on his BrainCare Score.

Prof. Visar Berisha Prof. Venet Osmani Prof. Fekrije Selimi Dr. Taulant Muka Dr. Jonathan Rosand Dr. Bardhë Agaj Dr. Egzona Morina
event · 2022

start a career in data science 2022

jun 27, 2022 5:00 pm bst microsoft reactor, london 171 attendees

In collaboration with our friends from the Data Science Initiative ALSA network, we organized a one-day conference hosting experts in Data Science, while giving those interested in pursuing a career in Data Science the opportunity to explore and inquire about how to do so successfully.

event · 2020

neuroscience for kosovo 2020

may 12, 2020 2:45 pm bst crowdcast (online) 200 attendees

Organizing Neuroscience for Kosova, we built on the emerging success of online conferences while simultaneously using these venues to educate and mobilize support for critical communities.

The event had a $10 registration fee. ALL proceeds from the registration and additional open donations were used to support Kosovo's COVID-19 relief program and XhMF's BrainCamp Kosovo.

Prof. Matteo Carandini Prof. Kenneth Harris Prof. Kishore Kuchibhotla Prof. Mackenzie Mathis Prof. Sara Solla Prof. Gina Turrigiano Prof. Robert Froemke Prof. Annegret Falkner
